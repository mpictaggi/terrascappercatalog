Leaf Detail
Leaf ShapeOvate to elliptic, slightly asymmetric
Leaf ColorDark green with silver vein patterning
Leaf Size3–6 cm; patterning most vivid in bright light
StemFleshy, reddish-pink; creeping & rooting

TextureSmooth, slightly succulent feel
Compatible With
Dart FrogsCrested GeckosIsopodsSpringtailsMosses & Ferns
Propagation

Stem cuttings root readily — press a node-bearing section onto moist substrate or sphagnum. New roots appear within 1–2 weeks in warm, humid conditions.

⚠ Important Notes
•Overwatering causes stem rot — ensure good drainage in substrate layer.
•Direct sun bleaches the silver patterning; always use indirect light.
