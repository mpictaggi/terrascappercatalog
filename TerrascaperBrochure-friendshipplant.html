Leaf Detail
Leaf ShapeOvate; deeply quilted/bullate texture
Leaf ColorBronze-green with silver veining
Leaf Size3–6 cm; pairs opposite on stem
StemSucculent, reddish; low spreading
Root TypeFine fibrous; surface rooting
FloweringTiny white/pink; insignificant
Compatible With
MossesFittoniaMini FernsPeperomiaDartfrogs
Propagation

Take stem cuttings 5–8 cm long just below a node. Roots form within 2–3 weeks in high humidity.

⚠ Important Notes
•Overwatering causes stem rot — allow surface to partially dry.
•Direct sunlight bleaches the patterned leaves.
